Frequently Asked Questions

What is Smurf Finder and how does it work?
Smurf Finder is a tool designed to identify smurf accounts in competitive games. It works by analyzing player behavior patterns, match history, and performance metrics to detect experienced players using new accounts.
Is my data safe with Smurf Finder?
Yes, we take data privacy very seriously. We only collect the minimum data needed to provide our services and never share your personal information with third parties. You can read more in our Privacy Policy.
How accurate is Smurf Finder?
Smurf Finder has an accuracy rate of approximately 85-90%. We continuously improve our algorithms based on user feedback and new data patterns to increase this accuracy over time.
How can I provide feedback or report a bug?
You can send us feedback or report bugs using the contact form on this page or by emailing us directly at SmurfFinderContactUs@gmail.com. We appreciate all feedback as it helps us improve our service!
