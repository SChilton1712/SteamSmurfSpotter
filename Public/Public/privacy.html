Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 5, 2025

At Smurf Finder, your privacy is of utmost importance to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our services. Please read this policy carefully to understand our views and practices regarding your personal data and how we will treat it.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect and process the following types of data:

  • Account Information: Such as your username, email address, or any information you voluntarily submit when interacting with features on our site.
  • Steam ID and Public Game Data: When provided, we access your public Steam profile information through the Steam Web API.
  • Technical Data: Includes IP address, browser type, device identifiers, operating system, referring URLs, and site interaction data.
  • Cookies and Analytics: We use cookies and similar technologies to collect usage data, remember preferences, and improve performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data helps us to:

  • Provide core functionality like smurf detection and Steam data lookups
  • Customize and enhance your user experience
  • Send important service-related communications
  • Monitor platform security and prevent misuse
  • Analyze site usage to improve features and content

3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data based on one or more of the following legal bases:

  • Your explicit consent (e.g., accepting cookies)
  • Performance of a contract (e.g., when you use our services)
  • Compliance with legal obligations
  • Our legitimate interests in maintaining and improving the site

4. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. After this period, your data will be securely deleted or anonymized.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. However, we may share data with:

  • Trusted third-party service providers who help us operate the website and services (e.g., analytics, hosting)
  • Legal authorities if required to comply with applicable laws or legal processes
  • Successors in the event of a merger, acquisition, or business transfer

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Cookies help us understand how you use our website. We use both session and persistent cookies for functionality, preferences, and analytics. You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings, but some features may become unavailable.

7. Your Rights

You have rights under data protection laws, which include the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of any inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict processing
  • Withdraw consent at any time where applicable
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format

If you wish to exercise any of these rights, contact us at contact@smurffinder.com.

8. Data Security

We take data protection seriously. We use encryption, access controls, and secure communication protocols to safeguard your information. However, please be aware that no transmission over the internet is 100% secure.
